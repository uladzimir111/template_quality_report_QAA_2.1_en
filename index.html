General quality analysis
- The level of readiness of the application provided the opportunity to test the functionality of the Dashboard and Action Plans modules. The aplication was tested using Google Chrome 102.0 and Mozilla Firefox 48.0 browsers. Modules Dashboard and Action Plans were tested according to the Test Survey prepaired by our Team members. The total number of defects found by our team is 52, of which 29 defects are Functional, 22 concern GUI of the application and 1 concerns the Specification. The severity level for 10 defects is Critical, while 11 defects have Major and 10 have Average severety.

- Running the Application on Firefox 48.0 reveals a lot of missing data and unfinished modules resulting in Low quality mark. The number of defects found when running on Firefox 48.0 is 18, with 6 Critical and 6 Major issues.

- When using more popular Google Chrome 102.0 the application performs a bit better and the quality can be estimated as Below Average. The number of defects found using Chrome 102.0 is 39, with 4 Critical and 4 Major issues.

- The overall quality of the application can be estimated as Below Average. A detailed quality analysis is presented below.

General and GUI
•  Medium
A significant number of inconsistencies between GUI and specification requirements was identified. There were many issues found, including mismatch of section names, incorrect time and date formats, as well as lack or excess of data. At the same time, GUI issues had a smaller impact on the assessment of the quality of the application, compared to the identified functional issues. Inoperability of a number of functions and invalid data did not allow testing the application in the full planned scope. The quality of application work in Google Chrome application is higher compared to the Mozilla Firefox browser.
 

Dashboard
•  Low
Test results for this module vary depending on the environment used. The majority of defects were found there. The total number of the module issues is 25 with Google Chrome, including 3 Critical, 3 Major, 4 Average, 15 Minor issues. The quality of this module in Google Chrome environment can be estimated as Below Average. The total number of the module issues is 9 with Mozilla Firefox, including 4 Critical, 2 Major, 1 Average, 2 Minor. Although there are fewer problems when testing with Mozilla Firefox, they are more serious and completely blocked the testing of many functions. The main defects are caused by the lack of information (QATC-604051, QATC-604048, QATC-603455) and broken links (QATC-603509, QATC-603515).
